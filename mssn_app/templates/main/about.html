ABOUT MSSN OAUSTECH

Muslim Students' Society of Nigeria (MSSN) at Olusegun Agagu University of Science and Technology (OAUSTECH) was established in 2015 (1436 AH) with a vision to inspire, unite, and empower the Muslim student community, MSSN OAUSTECH stands as a beacon of Islamic values, academic excellence, and community service within the university.

Mission

Our mission is to foster a vibrant Islamic community, promote academic success, and serve as a catalyst for positive change on campus and beyond. We strive to empower students with the knowledge, skills, and values necessary to thrive academically, spiritually, and socially.

Vision

Our vision is to be a dynamic and inclusive student organization that inspires unity, cultivates leadership, and promotes excellence in all aspects of student life. We envision a campus community where Islamic values are embraced, diversity is celebrated, and every student is empowered to reach their full potential.

Activities and Programs

At MSSN OAUSTECH, we offer a wide range of activities, programs, and services to enrich the academic, spiritual, and social lives of our members. From Usroh classes and dawah nights to Arabic classes and community service projects, our diverse offerings cater to the needs and interests of all students.

Impact

Since our establishment, MSSN OAUSTECH has made a significant impact on campus and in the wider community. Through our various initiatives and projects, we have touched the lives of countless students, promoted Islamic values, and contributed positively to the university's academic and cultural landscape.

Get Involved

Whether you're a new student looking to connect with like-minded peers or a returning member seeking to deepen your engagement, there are numerous opportunities to get involved with MSSN OAUSTECH. Join us in our mission to inspire, unite, and empower the Muslim student community at OAUSTECH!
