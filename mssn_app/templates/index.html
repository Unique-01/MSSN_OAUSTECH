About

The Muslim Students Society of Nigeria (MSSN), Olusegun Agagu University of Science and Technology, Okitipupa is the only Islamic organization in OAUSTECH Campus which is meant to cater for the welfare of Muslim Students on Campus. The body provides academic, financial, moral as well as upliftment for Muslim Students so they can enjoy their stay during their time on Campus.
